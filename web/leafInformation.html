• Context
    Attributes that concern the history of the manuscript, including shelfmarks, current location, patronage, provenance, additions to the manuscript that point to function and ownership, including the binding, ex libris plates, library stamps, insignia, frames and other evidence of use.
  • Carrier
    Attributes that concern the description of the manuscript as a physical artifact, including date, place of origin, support, dimensions, script, layout, decoration, scribes, and artists.
  • Content
    Attributes that concern the text or verbal information of the manuscript, including author, title, subject, language, translator, prologue, incipits, explicits, colophons, and indices.
  • General
    Attributes that the Contributor does not wish to assign to any category. This section can also be for general notes or for interesting information you would like to store with the object you are describing (such as a Digital Scriptorium link to a record for this object)
  • Arrange
    This area shows the sections into which you can place the leaf. By default, the leaf you are creating has already been placed into the "bucket", which is just a collection of all the leaves contributed thus far. If you would like to put it into a specific section, click on that section to highlight it. Click "Save Arrangement" to save your selection.
